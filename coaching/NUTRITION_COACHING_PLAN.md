# Nutrition Coach Study Plan
> Focus: Sports-Active Parents | Fat Loss | Aerobic Training

## Overview

This plan builds nutrition coaching expertise across 4 phases over ~6 months, moving from
foundational science → exercise nutrition → parent-specific coaching → certification & practice.

---

**Goal:** Build the scientific base. Understand how food works in the body.

**Weekly commitment:** 5–7 hrs/week

## Phase 4 — Certification & Practice (Weeks 19–26)

**Goal:** Apply knowledge, get certified, start coaching real clients.

**Weekly commitment:** 8–10 hrs/week

### Certification

- **Precision Nutrition Level 1 (PN1)** — gold standard for nutrition coaching, no prior degree needed
  - Website: [precisionnutrition.com](https://www.precisionnutrition.com)
