## Part 2 — Coach Pre-Call Prep (5 Min)

- [ ] Read check-in; mark **one** friction theme.  
- [ ] Note **one** win to name specifically.  
- [ ] Decide: **continue**, **tweak**, or **simplify** plan.  
- [ ] Prepare **one SMART process goal** + **Plan B**.

---

## Part 3 — 15–20 Minute Call Script (Coach)

### Minute 0–2 — Opening

> “Hey — good to see you. Before we dive in, **anything big** in life or training I should know?”

*(Listen; acknowledge.)*

### Minute 2–5 — Metrics (Neutral, Trend)

> “Thanks for sending numbers. We’re looking at **direction**, not single points. **Here’s what I see…**”

**If weight flat but behaviors improved:**  
> “This is where a lot of people quit — **your process is doing the right work.** We stay the course or adjust **one** variable.”

### Minute 5–10 — Behaviors: Training + Nutrition

> “Walk me through **which days felt on-plan** vs **which slid** — **curiosity**, not a report card.”

**Probes:**

- “What happened **the hour before** that choice?”  
- “Was it **hunger, time, emotion, or social**?”

### Minute 10–14 — Wins + One Problem

> “I want to name a win: **________**. That matters because **________**.”

> “If we fix **one thing** next week, what would move the needle most for **you**?”

**Coach selects *one* lever** (not five).

### Minute 14–18 — Next Week’s Focus + Plan B

> “So the experiment this week is: **________** — specific enough that we both know if you did it.”

> “If that falls apart, **Plan B** is **________** so you’re never starting from zero.”

**Confirm SMART:**  
> “How **confident** are you that’s doable — 0–10?” *(If <7, shrink.)*

### Minute 18–20 — Close

> “Quick recap: **Win** = ___; **Focus** = ___; **Plan B** = ___. **Anything I missed?**”

> “Same time next week — send check-in by **[day/time]**.”

## Coach Phrase Bank (Fat Loss + Aerobic Parents)

| Situation | Line |
|-----------|------|
| Underfueling signals | “Let’s protect **Tuesday’s long effort** — we might need **more carb around that session**, not less food overall.” |
| Perfectionism | “We’re not grading **moral worth** — we’re adjusting **logistics**.” |
| Scale noise | “Weight **lags** behavior; we’re coaching **inputs** you control.” |
| Social weekend | “We **planned** for humans who have birthdays. Monday isn’t a **punishment** day — it’s **default anchors back**.” |
